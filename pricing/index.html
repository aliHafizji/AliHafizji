Hire Me

If you're looking for someone who has technical knowledge with a track record of delivering quality products and understands your business, then look no further and get in touch.

Process

Consultation

We get on a call and discuss the project.

Proposal

I'll send you a proposal based on our discussion including time-lines and cost.

Design

I'm not a designer so I'll wait for the design specs from your side.

Development & Testing

I follow the agile process and work in weekly sprints. I will send out a build and demo the work at the end of each sprint.

Deployment

After completing all the features I shall deploy the application to the appstore.

Maintenance

I offer a monthly retainer where I shall work on the application for 15 hours/month. The retainer will be at a discount to the hourly price.

FAQs

  • How do you charge?

    • Although I prefer to charge a fixed hourly rate, in cases where the budget is a constraint (yes I’m talking to you startups!) I have a different model where I charge a fixed monthly price. This, of course, is subject to the type of work involved and the duration of the project. I usually prefer to take up long term projects of a minimum duration of three months or having a budget of atleast $7000.

  • Our timezones are drastically apart. How will our engagement work?

    • Most of my clients are in the United States of America, which is 12 hours behind me and yet we've have excellent communication. I work during PST work hours and I make sure that I'm available for at-least four hours of your work day.

    I use Slack, Google Hangouts, Github and Screenhero to make the communication effective. I like to have daily stand-ups as well.

  • Are you open for long terms engagements (upwards of 3 months)?

    • I would love to engage in a long term contract. It is extremely fulfilling for me to see a project I've worked on blossom as the company grows and I enjoy being a part of the process.

  • I've got a deadline coming up soon. Can you help?

    • As much as I would love to help you out, please keep in mind that it takes time to build quality products and I don't like to compromise on quality.

  • I already have an application. Can you develop a new feature for me?

    • Yes I'm always up for a new challenge. However, please keep in mind that I will need some time to familiarize myself with the code. For engagements which are lesser than a month I charge by the hour.

  • I don't have a design. Can you also design the application?

    • Unfortunately, while I'm still learning how to design, I'm no expert. However, I can introduce you to a few good designers I've worked with.

  • Are you available to work from our offices?

    • I'd love to! It would be awesome to meet the team. However, this stance may vary depending upon my prior commitments or in cases of emergency.

  • Are you open to write a book for our publication?

    • Yes I am interested in writing more books. However, I need a minimum commitment of 10,000$ in sales from the publisher with a certain amount paid in advance.
